# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want this project to be useful, welcoming, and safe for people who are learning,
experimenting, and contributing from different backgrounds.

Examples of behavior that help:

- being respectful and patient
- assuming good intent
- giving actionable technical feedback
- disagreeing with ideas without attacking people
- helping newcomers get unstuck

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or intimidation
- personal attacks or repeated hostile behavior
- publishing someone else's private information
- dismissive gatekeeping or bad-faith disruption

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, follow the support path in
[SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md). Please avoid posting sensitive personal details in public.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributions that
harm the health of the project community.
